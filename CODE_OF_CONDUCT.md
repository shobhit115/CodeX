# Code of Conduct — CodeX Club

We want CodeX to be an open, welcoming, and respectful community. All contributors, maintainers, and community members are expected to follow these guidelines.

## Be Considerate

- Treat everyone with respect. Be thoughtful and kind in interactions.

## Be Collaborative

- Provide constructive feedback and assume good intent.

## Be Inclusive

- Avoid exclusionary language and respect diverse backgrounds and perspectives.

## Reporting Unacceptable Behavior

- If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers: `security@codex.club` (or reach out directly to the club's administrative team).
- Reports will be treated confidentially and handled according to the project's policies.

For the full text of the Contributor Covenant, see: [https://www.contributor-covenant.org/](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/)
